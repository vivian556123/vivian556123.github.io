Hello & Welcome!

Hi, I am Xinkai Wang. Currently, I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I am honored to be guided by Prof. Chao Li in Sustainable Architecture and Infrastructure Lab (SAIL). My research interests includes datacenter optimization and cloud-native applications. I am currently working on enhancing architectural observability of datacenters.
